Our Services

End-to-end workforce solutions designed to help you hire better, faster, and smarter across every stage of talent acquisition.

Permanent Staffing

Find the right full-time talent that aligns with your company culture and long-term goals. We manage the entire recruitment lifecycle from sourcing to onboarding, ensuring every hire is a perfect fit for your organization.

  • End-to-end recruitment management
  • Culture-fit assessment & interviews
  • Background verification & onboarding

Contract Staffing

Scale your workforce flexibly with our contract staffing solutions. Perfect for project-based needs, seasonal demands, and temporary roles requiring immediate deployment with full compliance coverage.

  • Rapid deployment within 48 hours
  • Full statutory compliance (PF/ESIC)
  • Contract-to-hire conversion options

Executive Search

Discreet, thorough, and results-driven executive search for C-suite and senior leadership roles. We identify visionary leaders aligned with your culture who can drive transformational growth.

  • CXO & Board-level search
  • Confidential talent mapping
  • Leadership assessment & due diligence

RPO Services

End-to-end Recruitment Process Outsourcing to streamline your hiring operations. Our dedicated teams manage sourcing, screening, interviews, and offer management as an extension of your HR.

  • Dedicated hiring teams on-site/off-site
  • SLA-driven delivery models
  • Real-time reporting & analytics

Payroll & Compliance

Comprehensive payroll outsourcing and statutory compliance management. We handle PF, ESIC, professional tax, TDS, and all labor law requirements so you remain fully protected.

  • Error-free payroll processing
  • 100% statutory compliance
  • Self-service portals & MIS reports

HR Consulting & Talent Advisory

Strategic workforce advisory to identify talent gaps, build hiring roadmaps, and optimize your human capital. From policy formulation to DE&I strategies, we provide expert guidance.

  • Workforce planning & analytics
  • Compensation benchmarking
  • DE&I strategy & implementation
